Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I check If I am eligible for Instant Lending?

To apply, you must be at least 18 years old, a UK resident and have a UK bank account and debit card. You must also be employed, with a regular paycheuqe, and have a mobile phone. Get your application started by requesting a callback from us and we will fill the form for you!

2. Will I be accepted with Instant Lending before I apply?

Yes! you will be accepted with whatever credit history you have. Here at Instant Lending will find a loan which is most suitable for you. Instant Lending gives 100% guareented loan approval.

3. How do I apply for a Instant Lending loan?

All you have to so is fill out the Request Enquiry form or a Callback Request form and we will give you a call in your prefeered time. It only takes 2 minutes to fill out the form from our side with your basic information.

4. Can I reborrow even if I already have a loan with Instant Lending?

Yes! you can reborrow from us for the second time all you have to do is follow the same process for applying fill out the Request Enquiry form or a Callback Request form and we will look up your deatils and current loan repayments and make a desicion.

5. What is the amount I can borrow from Instant Lending?

With Instant Lending you can borrow from £100 to £10,000. Each loan is for a fixed term and different reapyment options depending your circumstances. But our flexible overpayment plans makes it easy to repay early. If you would like to get an idea of how much you will repay (and save) if you repay early request a callback and we will answer your questions.

6. Will I be credit checked when I apply?

Yes, as part of our commitment to responsible lending, we carry out credit checks on all new applications submitted to us. This assists with fraud prevention and also ensures we only approve applications from people who we believe will be able to repay what they borrow. This does not take long this is a very quick process we carry out so that you can get your loan as fast as possible!

7. When will the money be in my bank account?

Once approved, we will send your money within 20 minutes. If your bank accepts Faster Payments, then you may have access to the money straight away, but it depends on how quickly your bank processes deposits.
