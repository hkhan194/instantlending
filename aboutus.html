About Instant Lending



Instant Lending is made up of a number of international financial services businesses.

We are best known for our Instant short term loan product.

At Instant Lending we will find you a loan , even if your credit score isn’t perfect. Enquire about your loan with us and our fast, easy application could mean you have £100-£10,000 in your bank within 24 hours

We assure 100% gurranted loan approval to any customers who matches your eligibility


We launched a lending service in the UK that people could access online, tailor to their needs and understand the cost of upfront. The concept proved popular and we went on to process millions of applications. Our products are designed to provide responsible solutions for the everyday financial needs of modern life, especially for people who are not well served by mainstream finance.

Instant Lending based in London UK, offers solutions in the personal finance market. Established in 2012 we believe in offering customers a better way to shop for finance than the standard comparison model. We don’t offer headline rates to those customers who won’t qualify for them.
